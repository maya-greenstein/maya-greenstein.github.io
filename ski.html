Ski Jump Simulator

Description: For my senior project, my team was approached by US Nordic requesting that we create a ski jump simulator. There were not many guidelines, simply a problem that they had undreliable training methods. My team built a platform that was controlled by pnematic actuators that synced up with a VR headset displaying a ski jump. Analysis proved that most of the forces felt during a ski jump are in the body z direction, allowing a scaled version to be enacted by the lift.
